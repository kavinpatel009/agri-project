Tomato Farming

Tomato farming involves selecting the right variety, preparing well-drained soil rich in organic matter, and ensuring proper spacing for healthy growth. Tomatoes require full sunlight, regular watering, and support structures like stakes or cages. Fertilization with balanced nutrients and pest control are essential for high yields. Harvesting is done when the fruits reach their desired ripeness, usually 60–90 days after planting. 🍅🚜
