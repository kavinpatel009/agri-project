Fertilizers and Benefits

Select the appropriate fertilizer based on your soil health analysis and crop requirements

Urea

Nitrogen-Based Fertilizer

Urea is a source of Nitrogen, an essential nutrient crucial for crop growth and development. Urea is the most important nitrogenous fertilizer in the country because of its high N content (46%N)

Best For: Rice, Wheat, Maize, Sugarcane and Vegetables
Average Price: 1220 per 50kg
Application: Mix into soil before planting
Benefits: Improves soil structure, slow-release nutrients

Ammonium Nitrate

Nitrogen Fertilizer

Ammonium nitrate (NH₄NO₃) is a white, crystalline, water-soluble compound used as a fertilizer and a component in explosives. It's a strong oxidizer and can be dangerous if mishandled, especially when mixed with fuel.

Best For: Maize, Barley, Potatoes, Vegetables
Average Price ₹35,000–₹50,000 per metric ton
Application: Top dressing or dissolved in water
Benefits: Quick nitrogen release, making it more efficient for faster crop growth.

Diammonium Phosphate

Phosphorus Fertilizer

A widely used phosphorus fertilizer containing nitrogen that promotes root development and floweringDiammonium phosphate Diammonium phosphate (DAP) is a water-soluble fertilizer that contains nitrogen and phosphorus. It's the most widely used phosphorus fertilizer in the world. .

Best For: Fruit crops, flowering plants
Average Price ₹8,000–₹15,000 per metric ton
Application: Basal application at planting
Benefits: Strong root development, improves flowering Provides phosphorus and nitrogen to plants, stimulates root growth, and improves fruit formation

Muriate of Potash (MOP)

Potassium Fertilizer

A potassium-rich fertilizer essential for fruit development, disease resistance, and water regulationEnhances root strength and improves plant resistance to drought and diseases. Increases crop yield and enhances fruit quality.

Best For: Sugarcane, Potatoes, Bananas, Cotton, Oilseeds
Avrage Price: ₹34,000–₹42,000 per metric ton
Application: Side dressing during growth
Benefits: Improves fruit quality, enhances drought resistance

Sulfate of Potash (SOP)

Potassium-Based Fertilizer

A complete fertilizer containing nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in balanced proportions Suitable for chloride-sensitive crops as it has lower salt content than MOP. Improves flowering and fruit setting, enhances crop resistance to stress..

Best For: Most field and garden crops Tea, Tobacco, Grapes, Tomatoes, Citrus Fruits
Average Price: ₹50,000–₹70,000 per metric ton
Application: Basal and top dressing
Benefits: Complete nutrition, convenient application Provides sulfur, which is crucial for protein synthesis in plants.

NPK Fertilizers

NPK

Contains living microorganisms that enhance nutrie nt availability through natural processes NPK fertilizers are fertilizers that contain the elements nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K). Why these elements? Well, each element has been found to improve the health and appearance of plants..

Best For: Corn, Rice, Soybean, Vegetables
Average Price: 100-1500 per 20Kg
Application: Seed treatment or soil application
Benefits: Eco-friendly, improves soil health
